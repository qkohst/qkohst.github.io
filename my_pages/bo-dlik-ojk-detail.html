Backoffice DLIK OJK

This website is a Learning Management System (LMS) designed to manage training programs, webinars, and participant ranking summaries based on their activities. The system provides learning tracking, training needs analysis, and statistical reports for administrators.

Key Features:
  • Highest Ranking Dashboard
    • Displays participant rankings based on their training activities.
    • Includes details such as User Name, Training Period, Email, Total Points, Rewards, and Frequency.
    • Features search and filtering options based on training periods.
    • Allows users to export data in Excel and CSV formats.
  • Training and Webinar Management
    • Facilitates e-learning training management.
    • Admins can organize training materials in various formats such as videos, documents, and interactive quizzes.
    • Users can participate in webinars managed within the system.
    • A webinar dashboard is available to track participant statistics and webinar effectiveness.
  • Learning Points & Rewards
    • The system awards learning points to participants based on their engagement in training.
    • Participants can redeem rewards such as merchandise (T-shirts, bags, pouches, etc.) based on accumulated points.
  • Management Template
    • Admins can create and manage templates for various purposes, such as report generation or training certificates.
  • Chat & Forum
    • An interactive feature that allows users to discuss, ask questions, and share insights related to training and webinars.
  • Notifications & User Profile
    • Users receive notifications about training schedules and webinars.
    • Each participant has a user profile page storing personal information and training history.
