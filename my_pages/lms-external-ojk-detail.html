LMS External OJK

This website is a Learning Management System (LMS) that provides various training modules and webinars for users. It is designed to help participants understand materials through interactive learning modules and offers rewards based on their engagement in learning activities.

Key Features:
  • Learning Module Dashboard
    • Displays a list of recommended modules.
    • Users can search and filter modules based on categories or ratings.
    • Each module includes details such as the number of participants, ratings, and completion statistics.
  • Training & Webinar Management
    • Offers various learning materials in the form of videos, documents, and interactive quizzes.
    • Users can join webinars organized by the admin.
    • A webinar dashboard is available to track participant statistics and evaluate webinar effectiveness.
  • Points & Rewards System
    • Participants earn learning points based on their engagement.
    • Points can be redeemed for rewards such as exclusive merchandise (T-shirts, bags, etc.).
  • Module Search & Filter
    • Users can search for modules by title or category.
    • The A-Z sorting feature makes it easier to find desired modules.
  • Certification & Template Management
    • Admins can manage certificates for participants who complete certain modules.
    • A template management feature is available for creating and managing reports.
  • Discussion Forum & Chat
    • Allows participants to interact, discuss, and share insights about training modules.
    • Includes notifications for webinar schedule updates and training reminders.
  • User Profile
    • Each participant has a personal profile page to track their learning history, module progress, and accumulated points.
